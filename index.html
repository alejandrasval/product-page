Coffee Dolce Gusto

We are a coffee roasting company with 40 years of experience and a pioneer in the development of products derived from the famous bean in Quindío, Colombia. In this department, enriched and classified cherries are harvested with special techniques that have made Coffee Dolce Gusto a prestigious brand in the palates of coffee connoisseurs.

About us

Vision

In 2020, we will be a company positioned and recognized nationally and internationally for producing 100% premium coffees of Colombian origin, products derived from coffee and for the experience of our consumers in coffee shops.

Mision

With social and environmental responsibility to process high quality 100% Colombian coffee and products derived from the famous beverage, promoting coffee culture, generating employment and added value to coffee.
